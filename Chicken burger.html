CHICKEN BURGER

Chicken Burger

Chicken Burger: People's favourite one is always a chicken burger.

Ingredients

  • 2 halved burger buns
  • 500 gm minced chicken
  • 2 medium cheese slices
  • 4 pieces lettuce leaf
  • 1/2 gm sliced tomato,onion
  • All-Purpose Flour-80-100 gm
  • Lemon Juice- 2-3 tbsp
  • Tomato Sauce- 3-4 tbsp
  • Mayonnaise- 1 tbsp/li>
  • Peri-Peri Masala- 1 tbsp/li>
  • Mix of all spices
  • 100 ml milk
  • pinch of salt,chilli powder,Breadcrumbs

Procedure

  1. Prepare the chicken patties

    Take a bowl and combine minced chicken, bread crumbs, milk, chopped green onions, Worcestershire sauce and salt and pepper in a bowl. Then, form into patties.

  2. Grill or fry the patties or barbeque them until cooked properly.
  3. Slice vegetables for the burger and slice burger buns into halves.
  4. Add slices of veggies, lettuce leaf and chicken patty on one-half of the burger bun and cover it with the other half on the bun. Enjoy!
  5. Chicken Burger is ready. Enjoy it with fries and chilled drinks.
