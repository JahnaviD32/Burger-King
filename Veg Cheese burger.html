VEG CHEESE BURGER

Veg Cheese Burger

Cheese Burger is a snack with loads of flavorful vegetables in a bun. It is a popular street food originated in the United States.

Ingredients

  • 2 halved burger buns
  • 1/2 finely chopped capsicum ( green pepper)
  • 2 medium cheese slices
  • 1/2 gm sliced tomato,onion
  • 80 gm shredded gobi,carrot,beans,peas
  • 4 pieces lettuce leaf
  • Mix of all spices
  • pinch of salt,chilli powder,Breadcrumbs

For the Potato tikki dish:

  1. 3 medium boiled,mashed,peeled potato

Procedure

  1. Firstly, steam ½ carrot, 6 gobi, 4 beans, 2 tbsp sweet corn, 2 tbsp peas and ¼ tsp salt.
  2. Steam for 10 minutes on medium flame.Transfer the steamed vegetables to large mixing bowl.
  3. Add 3 boiled and mashed potato.Further, add ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp garam masala, ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp chaat masala and ½ tsp salt.
  4. Add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well combining all the spices well.Add ¼ cup bread crumbs and mix well. breadcrumbs help to absorb moisture from vegetables
  5. Prepare a slurry by mixing 2 tbsp cornflour, ¼ cup maida and ½ cup water in a small bowl.prepare ball sized patties and dip in maida slurry.coat with panko breadcrumbs uniformly.
  6. Shallow fry in hot oil keeping the flame on medium.Fry on both sides, until it turns golden brown.Drain off and keep aside..
  7. Place everything in order to finish tasty veg cheese burger
  8. Veg Cheese Burger is ready. Enjoy it with fries and chilled drinks.
